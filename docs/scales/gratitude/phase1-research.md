## Construct Definition

**Gratitude（感謝）**は、VIA Classification of Character Strengthsにおける24の性格的強みの一つで、**Transcendence（超越）**という徳（virtue）に分類されます。

**定義**: "Awareness and expressing thankfulness for the good things in life"
（人生の良いことに気づき、感謝を表現すること）

Gratitudeは、以下の要素を含みます：
- 受け取った良いことへの気づき
- 感謝の気持ちの体験
- 感謝の表現（言葉や行動で）
- 自分の祝福を数える習慣

## Evidence for Interventions

### Gratitude Interventions (Research-Backed)

**Three Good Things** (Most evidence):
- Write down 3 good things each day for 1-2 weeks
- Effect size: Small to medium on well-being
- Lasting effects: Up to 6 months

**Gratitude Letter**:
- Write and deliver a letter to someone who helped you
- Effect size: Medium on well-being (short-term)
- Lasting effects: 1-3 months

**Gratitude Journaling**:
- Weekly gratitude journal (3-5 entries per week)
- Effect size: Small on well-being
- More effective when done weekly vs. daily

**Effect Sizes (Meta-Analysis)**:
- Well-being: Hedges' g = 0.19 (small but meaningful)
- Depression/Anxiety: g = -0.29 (small to medium reduction)
