## Existing Japanese RIASEC Assessments

### 1. VPI職業興味検査 (VPI Vocational Preference Inventory)

**Developer**: Japan Institute for Labour Policy and Training (JILPT) / 労働政策研究・研修機構
**Publisher**: 日本文化科学社 (Nihon Bunka Kagakusha)
**Based on**: Holland's original VPI (1950s-1970s version)
**Items**: 160 occupational names
**Response Format**: Binary (Yes/No - "興味がある" / "興味がない")
**Administration Time**: ~20 minutes (including scoring)

**Translation Method**:
- Adapted by 渡辺三枝子 (Watanabe Mieko) and JILPT researchers
- Japanese version developed with extensive research on applicability of Holland's theory to Japanese populations
- Third edition (2002) re-standardized occupational names for contemporary relevance

**Psychometric Properties**:
- ✅ Validated for Japanese population
- ✅ Standardized on university/college students and working adults
- ❌ Specific Cronbach's α coefficients not publicly available
- ✅ Includes validity scales (defensive responding, acquiescence)

**License**: ❌ **Commercial** - Requires purchase from publisher

**Source**: [VPI職業興味検査｜JILPT](https://www.jil.go.jp/institute/seika/tools/VPI.html)

### 2. マイジョブ・カード RIASEC Interest Assessment

**Provider**: 株式会社リアセック (Riacsec Inc.)
**Platform**: 厚生労働省 マイジョブ・カード (MHLW My Job Card website)
**Items**: 30 questions
**Response Format**: 3-point Likert scale
- とても興味がある (Very interested)
- 興味がある (Interested)
- 興味がない (Not interested)

**Content Examples**:
- Technical/mechanical work (Realistic)
- Research and analysis (Investigative)
- Creative expression (Artistic)
- Service and helping (Social)
- Business and leadership (Enterprising)
- Administrative and organizational (Conventional)

**License**: ❌ **Proprietary** - Owned by Riacsec Inc.

**Source**: [興味診断 | マイジョブ・カード](https://www.job-card.mhlw.go.jp/shindan/riasec)

## Recommendation

⚠️ **Independent Translation of O*NET Interest Profiler 60-item Short Form**

### Rationale

1. **No free Japanese version exists** with comparable psychometric quality
2. **O*NET license permits adaptation** under open license
3. **VPI and マイジョブ・カード require commercial licenses**
4. **60 items is optimal length** for web-based self-assessment (~5-7 minutes)
5. **Strong psychometric foundation** (α = 0.78-0.90, validated hexagonal structure)

### Translation Strategy

**Method**: Back-translation (English → Japanese → English)

**Process**:
1. **Forward Translation** (English → Japanese)
   - Hire professional translator with psychology background
   - Translate all 60 work activity statements
   - Translate response scale labels (1 = Strongly Dislike → 5 = Strongly Like)

2. **Back-Translation** (Japanese → English)
   - Independent bilingual translator (blind to original)
   - Compare back-translation with original O*NET items
   - Revise Japanese version where discrepancies exist

3. **Expert Review**
   - Panel of 2-3 career counselors or I/O psychologists
   - Review for:
     - Cultural appropriateness
     - Clarity of work activity descriptions
     - Equivalence to original meaning

4. **Pilot Testing**
   - Administer to n ≥ 50 Japanese speakers
   - Calculate Cronbach's α for each RIASEC dimension
   - **Acceptance criteria**: α ≥ 0.75 for each dimension
   - If α < 0.75, revise problematic items and retest

5. **Validation (Optional but Recommended)**
   - Correlate with existing Japanese career interest measures (e.g., VPI if accessible)
   - Confirm hexagonal structure via confirmatory factor analysis (CFA)

### Expected Timeline

- Translation & back-translation: 2-3 weeks
- Expert review: 1 week
- Pilot testing: 2-4 weeks (recruitment + data collection)
- Analysis & revision: 1 week

**Total**: ~6-10 weeks

### Cost Estimate

- Professional translators (forward + back): ¥100,000-200,000
- Expert panel (honoraria): ¥50,000-100,000
- Pilot testing (participant incentives): ¥50,000
- **Total**: ~¥200,000-350,000

### Alternative: Use English Version

**Pros**:
- ✅ No translation cost
- ✅ Immediate implementation
- ✅ Known psychometric properties

**Cons**:
- ❌ Excludes non-English speakers
- ❌ May reduce accuracy for non-native speakers
- ❌ Limits target audience for psychtest.jp

**Verdict**: Not recommended unless targeting English-speaking Japanese residents
