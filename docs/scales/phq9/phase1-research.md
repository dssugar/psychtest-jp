## Psychometric Properties
- **Items**: 9
- **Response Format**: 4-point Likert scale (0-3)
  - 0 = まったくない (Not at all)
  - 1 = 数日 (Several days)
  - 2 = 半分以上 (More than half the days)
  - 3 = ほとんど毎日 (Nearly every day)
- **Score Range**: 0-27
- **Cronbach's α**: 0.89 (primary care), 0.86 (OB-GYN)
- **Meta-analytic pooled α**: 0.86 (95% CI [0.85, 0.87]) (n=232,147)
- **Test-Retest**: r = 0.84 (48 hours), pooled r = 0.82 (95% CI [0.74, 0.90])
- **Sample Size**: n = 6,000 (original validation)
- **Sensitivity/Specificity**: 88%/88% (cutoff ≥ 10)
- **Citations**: 20,000+ (highly cited, gold standard tool)
